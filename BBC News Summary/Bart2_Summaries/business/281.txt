 Regular bonus rates on former Axa Equity & Law with-profits policies to be cut from 2% to 1% for 2004 . Axa blamed poor stock market performance for the cut . The cut will hit an estimated 3% of Axa's policyholders . The rest will know their fate in March when they find out their fate . Heavy stock market falls throughout 2001 and 2002 forced most firms to trim bonus rates . With-profit policies are designed to smooth out the peaks and troughs of stock market volatility .
