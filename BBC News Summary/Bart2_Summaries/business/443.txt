 Kraft to cut back on advertising of products like Oreo and Kool-Aid . The move is aimed at children between the ages of six and 11 . It will also add a label to its more nutritional and low-fat brands to promote the benefits . The moves come as the firms face criticism from consumer groups concerned at rising levels of obesity in US children . Kraft rival PepsiCo also began a similar initiative last year to promote healthier eating in the US last year . The company says it will continue to advertise all its products in media seen by parents .
