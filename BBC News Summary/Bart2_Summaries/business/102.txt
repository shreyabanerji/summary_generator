 California-based company pleaded guilty to falsifying its accounts and violating US anti-bribery laws . It agreed to pay $13m in criminal penalties, as well as $15.5m to settle a civil lawsuit brought by the SEC . Some of the money was used to pay for T-shirts with campaign slogans on them ahead of the 2001 election . No suggestion that President Mathieu Kerekou was himself aware of any wrongdoing . At the time, Titan was trying to get a higher price for a telecommunications project in Benin .
