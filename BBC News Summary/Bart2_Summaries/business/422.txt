 News Corp makes $5.4bn Fox offer to buy out minority investors in Fox Entertainment Group . Rupert Murdoch owns 82% of the shares in the company, home to the Fox television network and the 20th Century Fox film studio . News Corp shares fell 25 cents to $17.65 after the share offer was announced . Fox shares were up 19 cents at $31.22.22 after the offer was made . The move follows News Corp's decision to register its business in the US .
