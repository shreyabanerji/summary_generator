 US firms added only 146,000 jobs in January, down from 190,000 expected . Jobless rate falls to 5.2%, its lowest level since September 2001 . President Bush can celebrate net growth in jobs in his first term in office . Analysts say growth in new jobs is not as strong as expected given favourable economic conditions given the low value of the dollar and still relatively low interest rate environment . But job creation became a key issue in last year's election as President Bush's first term jobs record ended in positive territory .
