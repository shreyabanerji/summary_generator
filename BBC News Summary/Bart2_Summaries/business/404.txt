 Continental Airlines warns of 'inadequate liquidity' if it fails to reduce wage costs by $500m by end of February . Shares in the fifth biggest US carrier fall 6.87% on the news to $10.44 by 1830 GMT . Company has managed to save $1.1bn in costs without cutting jobs . Company now has debt and pension payments of nearly $984m this year which it must pay off this year . Failure to make cutbacks may also push it to reduce its fleet .
