 Chile's copper industry registered record earnings of $14.2bn in 2004 . Industry expects to see investment worth $10bn over the next three years . BHP Billiton planning to invest $1.9bn between now and 2007 . Production in Chile expected to rise 3.5% in 2005 to 5.5 million tonnes . Copper price reached 16-year high in October 2004; export earnings expected to fall 10.7% for 2005 . Chile is now analyzing ways of to keep prices stable at current high levels .
