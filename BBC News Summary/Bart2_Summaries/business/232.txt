 Lloyd's chief executive urges FSA to force brokers to disclose size of commissions . FSA took over regulation of the general insurance sector in January . Brokers must give information on commissions if, and only if, their customers request it, the FSA said . In the US, lack of transparency about brokers' commissions has led to problems in the US . The world's biggest insurance broker Marsh & McLennan said last week it would pay $850m to settle charges that it sought to rig bids in conjunction with insurers .
