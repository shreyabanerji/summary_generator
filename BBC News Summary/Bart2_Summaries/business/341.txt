 Jarvis sells Tube stake to Spain's Ferrovial for £146m ($281m) Sale of 33% stake is a lifeline to Jarvis, which was weighed down by debts of more than £230m . Spanish group has been keen to snap up more UK infrastructure assets, having bought Amey in 2003 . Jarvis said sale would "substantially" enhance its financial position . Shares in Jarvis were up more than 16% to 18 pence by the close of trade on Friday .
