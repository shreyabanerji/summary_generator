 French consumers increased their spending by 1.5% in January . Consumer spending in January rose 3.8% on a year-on-year basis . Consumer prices in France fell 0.6% on an annual basis . Italian consumer confidence rose to 104.4 from 103.3, despite a slight deterioration in short-term sentiment . In Italy, consumer confidence has risen to its highest level since October 2004, according to ISAE research group ISAE . In France, consumer spending fuelled France's economic growth in the last quarter of 2004 .
