 EU ministers to discuss a proposed tax on jet fuel in Brussels on Thursday . Airlines and aviation bodies say it would hurt their earnings at a time when they are under pressure . France and Germany want to boost the amount of aid they give to 0.7% of their annual gross national income by 2015 . Ministers will also be discussing reforms to European regulations governing European public sector spending in the coming weeks on a range of topics including jet fuel taxes and taxes on airlines and other industries . The EU says a new tax would only be passed after full negotiations with the airlines .
