 Court rejects $280bn tobacco case against tobacco companies . Court in Washington found that the case could not be brought under federal anti-racketeering laws . Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia ruled that the government could not sue the firms under legislation drawn up to counteract Mafia infiltration of business . Altria Group, RJ Reynolds Tobacco, Lorillard Tobacco, Liggett Group and Brown and Williamson accused of covering up the effects of smoking . The tobacco companies deny that they illegally conspired to promote smoking and defraud the public .
