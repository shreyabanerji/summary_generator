 Fiat mulls Ferrari market listing and bringing Maserati and Alfa Romeo closer together, it has been reported . The Italian carmaker is set to restructure its business after reaching a $2bn settlement with GM about Fiat's ownership . Fiat owns a 56% stake in Ferrari -best known for its dominant Formula One motor racing team - having first bought into the business in 1969 . It considered floating Ferrari in 2002 but opted to sell a minority stake to Italian bank Mediobanca for $1bn .
