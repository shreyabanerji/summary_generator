 Dollar hits new low versus euro at $1.364 per euro at 1800 GMT on Monday . It has lost 7% this year, while against the Japanese yen it is down 3.2% . The US is struggling with a ballooning trade deficit and one of the easiest ways to fund it was by allowing a depreciation of the dollar . Analysts predict that the dollar is likely to fall even further in the coming days . The European Central Bank is thought to favour a declining dollar, while the US is expected to favour the euro .
