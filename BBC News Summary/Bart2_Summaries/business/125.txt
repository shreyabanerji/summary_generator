 WMC Resources profits up fivefold in 2004, up from A$246bn the year before . Australian government opposes takeover bid by Swiss company Xstrata . WMC has no objection in principle to being bought out, having spun off its aluminium operations in 2002 to make itself a more tempting target - as long as the price is right . Copper and nickel in particular have been in high demand thanks to China's booming economy, which expanded more than 9% in each of the past two years .
