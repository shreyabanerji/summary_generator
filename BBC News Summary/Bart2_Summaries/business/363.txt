 $1.2bn oil pipeline will run from Bulgarian port of Burgas to the Albanian city of Vlore on the Adriatic coast . Project aims to allow alternative ports for the shipping of Russian and Caspian oil, that normally goes through Turkish ports . The pipeline will be built by the US-registered Albanian Macedonian Bulgarian Oil Corporation (AMBO) It aims to transport 750,000 daily barrels of oil . The project was conceived in 1994 but it was delayed because of the lack of political support .
