The countries stand to reap huge benefits from membership of the group, whose purpose is to promote free trade . Joining, however, is a lengthy process . China's admission in 2001 took 15 years and talks with Russia and Saudi Arabia have been taking place for 10 years . There are now 27 countries seeking membership .
