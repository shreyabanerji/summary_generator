 UK banking group Barclays shares up on merger talk with US bank Wells Fargo . UK banking icon Barclays was founded more than 300 years ago; it has operations in over 60 countries and employs 76,200 staff worldwide . The two banks reportedly held talks in October and November 2004 . Barclays shares were up 8 pence, or 1.3%, at 605 pence by late morning in London on Monday, making it the second biggest gainer in the FTSE 100 index . Wells Fargo had net income of $6.2bn in its last financial year, a 9% increase on previous year .
