 Production dropped 1.6% in October, reflecting a decline in exports . Jobless figures edged up 0.1% to 4.7%, slightly higher than forecast . Japan's economy has grown for six quarters but growth slowed dramatically in the last quarter . Government officials play down the latest data, arguing domestic consumer demand is still resilient . The comparatively weak economic date preyed on shares with the Nikkei down 1% in afternoon trade . Japan downgraded its overall economic assessment earlier this month for the first time in a year .
