 Orange claims Easymobile has infringed its rights regarding the use of the colour orange . Easyjet founder Stelios Haji-Ioannou plans to launch Easymobile next month . Easymobile will allow customers to go online to order SIM cards and airtime for existing handsets . Orange says Easymobile could confuse customers - known as "passing off" - by using orange branding . Easygroup founder vows to fight back, saying: "We have nothing to be afraid of in this court case"
