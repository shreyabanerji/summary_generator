 Malaysia to relax restrictions on foreign ownership to allow Islamic banking . Restrictions on banks' licences will be lifted to 49% of the country's banks . The move comes ahead of a 2007 deadline to open up the market for the first time . Malaysia's economy grew by 5.6% in the first quarter of last year, according to the latest figures . The country's Islamic banking sector is now the world's largest and fastest growing economy . It is expected to grow by 6% this year, but the country has seen little growth .
