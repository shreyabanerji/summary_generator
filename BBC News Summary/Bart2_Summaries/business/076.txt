 Unemployment at 25%, compared with 10% before uprising four years ago . 104,000 new jobs created last year during easing of violence and closures . During first half of this year, Palestinian economy lost more than 22,000 jobs . Nearly half of all Palestinians - some 47% - live below the poverty line and are particularly vulnerable to economic shocks . Report says even more would be on poverty line without average of $950m a year from international donors, some of which goes towards job creation . US Secretary of State Colin Powell visiting West Bank to stress US support for smooth Palestinian election in January .
