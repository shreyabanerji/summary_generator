 Average UK house price fell from £180,126 in November to £178,906 . Land Registry figures confirm a slowdown in late 2004 . All major UK regions, apart from Northern Ireland, experienced a fall in annual growth during December . December is traditionally a quiet month for the housing market because of Christmas celebrations . Recent surveys from Nationwide and Halifax indicate market may be undergoing a revival. Halifax said house prices rose by 0.8% in January and Nationwide reported a rise of 0.4% in the first month of the year .
