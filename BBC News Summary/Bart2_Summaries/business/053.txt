 Revenues at media group Reuters slipped 11% from £3.24bn ($6.13bn) in 2003 to £2.89bn in 2004 . The company beat profit forecasts, posting a 52% rise in profits for the year to £198m from £130m seen a year earlier . Reuters also beat its savings target for 2004, delivering £234m of cuts . The news and financial data seller said the year had begun well, adding it expected "further gradual improvement" in the second quarter .
