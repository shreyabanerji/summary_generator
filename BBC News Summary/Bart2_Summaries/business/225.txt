 Sales in what is traditionally a strong month rose by 0.5% on a like-for-like basis . Consumers remain cautious over buying big-ticket items like furniture . December sales in December were the worst since 1981, according to Office for National Statistics . The previous BRC survey found Christmas 2004 was the worst for 10 years for retail sales in the UK since 1981 . But clothing and footwear sales were said to be generally better than December, while department stores also had a good month .
