 Pre-tax profit for 2004 was $29.4m . Firm recently lost sponsorship deals with Chelsea and Celtic . Firm hopes 2005 sales will benefit from launch of a new England replica shirt ahead of the 2006 World Cup . Umbro supplies more than 150 teams across the world including the national sides of Ireland, Sweden and Norway . Shares in Umbro were up 1.76% at 115.5 pence in morning trade on the London Stock Exchange in early trading hours on Thursday . It said the UK market had seen sales growth of 8% last year .
