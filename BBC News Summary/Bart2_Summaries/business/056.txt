 Luxury carmaker saw strong sales of its three marques - BMW, Mini and Rolls-Royce - in Asia last year . BMW-brand sales rose 2.3% to 80,600 while sales of Mini models rose 3.6% to 14,800 . BMW sold more than 100 of the iconic models compared with just ten the previous year . The German carmaker is aiming to boost annual sales in Asia to 150,000 by 2008 . China remains main area of concern for BMW after sales there fell 16% in 2004 .
