 China clamping down on credit will continue into 2005, state media report . Fears that too much stress might be placed on fragile banking system . Growth in China remains at a breakneck 9.1%, and corporate investment is growing at more than 25% a year . Low exchange rate means Chinese exports are cheap on world markets . Rapid tooling-up of manufacturing sector means a massive demand for energy - one of the factors which has kept world oil prices sky-high for most of this year .
