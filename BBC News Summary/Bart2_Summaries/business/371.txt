 Singapore's economy grew by 8.1% in 2004, its best performance since 2000 . Advance was the second-fastest in Asia after China . Economic growth is set to halve in Singapore this year to between 3% and 5% . The city state's gross domestic product rose at an annual rate of 2.4% in the fourth quarter, but was well below analyst forecasts . The main drag came from electronics, says JP Morgan Chase economist Lian Chia Liang, economist .
