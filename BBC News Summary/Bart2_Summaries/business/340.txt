 Venezuelan president Hugo Chavez has offered China wide-ranging access to the country's oil reserves . The deal will allow China to operate oil fields in Venezuela and invest in new refineries . Venezuela has also offered to supply 120,000 barrels of fuel oil a month to China . China's quick-growing economy's need for oil has contributed to record-high oil prices this year, along with political unrest in the Middle East and supply bottlenecks . China has been a net importer of oil since the mid 1990's with more than a third of the oil and gas it consumes coming from abroad . Venezuela sells about 60% of its output to the United States, which is trying to diversify sales to reduce its dependence on its largest export market .
