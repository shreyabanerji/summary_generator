 Creditors agreed on Friday to suspend interest payments until 2007 . News of the deal had boosted shares in the club on Friday, but the stock slipped back 7% during Monday morning . The football club is hoping that all its creditors will agree to defer rent payments on its Westfalen stadium . The club posted a record loss of 68m euros in the 12 months through June . Chief executive Gerd Niebaum stepped down last week and creditors have been pushing for a greater say in how the club is run .
