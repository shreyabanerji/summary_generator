 Mikhail Khodorkovsky handed over his entire 59.5% stake in Yukos to Leonid Nevzlin . Yukos' core oil production unit, Yuganskneftegaz, was sold to pay a giant tax bill . The former tycoon is currently on trial for fraud and tax evasion following his arrest in 2003 . He is widely considered to be Russia's richest man, with an estimated fortune of $15bn, but the charges are widely seen as politically motivated .
