 Average UK house price falls marginally to £180,226, from £179,444 in October . Halifax says house prices increased by 1.1% in December - first monthly rise since September . Bank of England says number of mortgages approved in UK has fallen to lowest level for nearly a decade . North East shows highest rate of inflation at 26.2%, followed by Yorkshire and Humber on 21.7%, and the North West at 21.1%. All areas saw a rise in annual house price inflation in November except for Northern Ireland and the West Midlands, where rate was unchanged, the Office of the Deputy Prime Minister said . In London, the area with the highest average house price at £262,825, annual inflation rose only slightly in November to 7% from 7% the previous month .
