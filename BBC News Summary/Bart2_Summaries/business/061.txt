 The number of people out of work rose 32,000 to 1.41 million in the last three months of 2004 . 90,000 more people were in employment in the same period . The UK's jobless rate rose to 4.7% from 4.6% in the previous quarter . However, the rate still remains one of the lowest in the world, compared with 12.1% in Germany, 10.4% in Spain and 9.7%. The figures prompted some analysts to forecast that the Bank of England will raise rates this year .
