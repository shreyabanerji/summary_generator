 Germany's unemployment rate rises to 10.8% for the first time since 1990 . Bad weather and a lack of work blamed for the rise . The rise is expected to continue to rise in coming months . New government reforms are expected to help reduce the number of jobless in Germany . The country's biggest economy has been hit by three years of economic downturn in the past decade, with unemployment down to 6.3% since 1990s, the government says . The figures are due to be released on Friday, January 1, 2015 .
