 CBI quarterly survey found output had risen by the fastest rate in seven years . Spending on innovation, training and retraining is forecast to go up over the next year . Some firms managed to increase prices for the first time in nine years - but many said increases failed to keep up the rise in costs . CBI said companies are looking to the government to lessen the regulatory load and are hoping interest rates will be kept on hold . The manufacturing sector needs a period of long-term stability in the economy, says the head of SME Council .
