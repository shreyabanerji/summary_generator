 General Electric posts 18% jump in sales and profits in fourth quarter of 2004 . CEO Jeff Immelt: "We are benefiting from our growth initiatives and an excellent global economy" Group forecast sustained growth at between 10-15% for this year and next . Full year 2004 profit was up 6% at $16.6bn, while sales came in at $43.7bn . Last year, GE bought Vivendi Universal, merging it with NBC to form NBC Universal . GE's shares rose 1% on the news before ending Friday 0.24% lower .
