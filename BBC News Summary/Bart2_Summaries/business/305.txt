 New law gives priority to workers, creditors and tax revenue . Law also makes it more difficult for a company to declare bankruptcy . New law will help reduce the spread between the interest rates of the banks and federal bonds . President Lula da Silva said the law was needed to reduce the cost of borrowing . The new law was proposed in 1993, but has now been approved by the president of Brazil's Congress . It was opposed by leftist parties, including Lula's Worker Party, who said it undermined workers' rights .
