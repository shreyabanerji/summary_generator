 China's demand for power has increased to 6.2 billion yuan in 2004 . The country has been hit by a number of problems with power supply . A total of 30 large-scale projects have been halted in China over fears of over-stimulating the market . The government is trying to reduce the number of people who want to see the country's power supply cut to 3 billion yuan a year ago, but that figure is expected to rise to 6,000 in 2013 . The Chinese government is also trying to curb demand for more power supplies .
