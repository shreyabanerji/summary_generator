 Deutsche Boerse has offered £1.3bn ($2.48bn; 1.88bn euros) for London Stock Exchange . Analysts say the move is aimed at winning over investors opposed to its bid for the London exchange . Critics of the takeover have complained that the money could be better used by returning cash to shareholders . Frankfurt-based exchange said profit in the three months to 31 December was 120.7m euros ($158.8m; £83.3m)
