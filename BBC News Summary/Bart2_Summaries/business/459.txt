 UK economy could suffer a backlash from the slowdown in the housing market . Deloitte is forecasting economic growth of 2% this year, below Gordon Brown's forecast of 3.5% . It expects a series of "aggressive" interest rates to fall to 4% by the end of the year . Economic growth is likely to be constrained during the next few years by increased pressure on household budgets and rising taxes, the firm says . But the Bank of England is expected to respond quickly to signs of faltering in the UK economy, it says . The UK economy has enjoyed a "golden period" during the past decade with unemployment falling to a near 30 year low and inflation at its lowest since the 1960s . Inflation is set to rise by about $10bn a year, the consultancy says.
