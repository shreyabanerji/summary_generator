 Lufthansa may sue over Bush visit to Frankfurt airport . Airline says it may lose millions of euros as a result of Air Force One landing . Bush's arrival at Frankfurt airport delayed flights for an hour, causing cancellations and delays . Company says it is not certain that it will take legal action against the German federal agencies that gave the orders that caused the delays, but may sue federal agencies for damages . Lufhansa accounts for six out of every 10 planes using Frankfurt's airport .
