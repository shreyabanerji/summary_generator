 The largest recall involves 155,465 pickups, vans and sports utility vehicles . This is because of possible malfunctions with the braking systems . The affected vehicles are from the 2004 and 2005 model years . GM stressed that it did not know of any injuries related to the problems . News of the recall follows an announcement last month that GM expects earnings this year be lower than in 2004 . The world's biggest car maker is grappling with losses in its European business, weak US sales and now a product recall .
