 Beirut's benchmark BLOM stock index closed down 7.9% at 642.80 . Solidere, in which Mr Hariri was a major shareholder, was the major drag on the index . The real estate firm owns much of the property in central Beirut, which it restored and redeveloped following the end of Lebanon's 15-year civil war . The company posted profits of $12.5m in the first half of 2004, and its shares had been gaining in recent months .
