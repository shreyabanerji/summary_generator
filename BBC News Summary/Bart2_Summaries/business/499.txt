 China Aviation Oil has offered to repay creditors $220m of the $550m it lost on trading in oil futures . Firm said it hoped to pay $100m now and another $120m over eight years . The firm had been betting heavily on a fall in the price of oil during October, but prices rose sharply instead . The trading scandal is the biggest to hit Singapore since the $1.2bn collapse of Barings Bank in 1995 . CEO Chen Jiulin was arrested by at Changi Airport by Singapore police on 8 December .
