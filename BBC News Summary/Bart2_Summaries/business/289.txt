 British Chamber of Commerce says UK manufacturing sector faces "serious challenges" Exports picked up in last three months of 2004 to their best levels in eight years . UK economy facing "major risks" and warned growth is set to slow, group says . Survey of 5,196 firms found domestic sales growth fell back slightly in the quarter . Employment in manufacturing also fell and job expectations were at their lowest level for a year, survey finds . The outlook for the service sector was "uncertain" despite an increase in exports and orders over the quarter, the BCC noted . It recently forecast economic growth will slow from more than 3% in 2004 to a little below 2.5% in both 2005 and 2006 . The Bank of England raised interest rates five times between November 2003 and August last year but rates have been kept on hold since then amid signs of falling consumer confidence and a slowdown in output ..
