 Holcim has bid $800m (£429m) to buy two Indian cement firms and a holding company . Shares in ACC fell 5.5% as investors thought the offer was underpriced . UK-based firm Aggregate Industries said it had agreed a £1.8bn takeover . Deal with Aggregates will give the world's second-biggest cement maker an entry into the UK market and boost its presence in the US and the UK . It will give HolcIM a major presence in India's fastest-growing market behind China .
