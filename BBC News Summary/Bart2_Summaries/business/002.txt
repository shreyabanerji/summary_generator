 Dollar hits highest level against euro in almost three months after Greenspan speech . Greenspan highlights US government's willingness to curb spending and rising household savings as factors which may help reduce deficit . G7 meeting unlikely to produce any meaningful movement in Chinese policy . China's currency remains pegged to the dollar and the US currency's sharp falls in recent months have made Chinese export prices highly competitive . But calls for a shift in Beijing's policy have fallen on deaf ears, despite recent comments in a major Chinese newspaper that the "time is ripe" for a loosening of the peg of the Chinese currency . The White House will announce its budget on Monday, and many commentators believe the deficit will remain at close to half a trillion dollars . The US Federal Reserve's decision on 2 February to boost interest rates by a quarter of a point could be enough to keep US assets looking more attractive, and could help prop up the dollar, some believe .
