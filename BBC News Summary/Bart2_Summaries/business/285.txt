 EU leaders pledged the European economy would outstrip that of the US by 2010 . Only the UK, Finland, Belgium, Denmark, Ireland and the Netherlands have followed up policy recommendations . The Lisbon Agenda set out to increase the number of people employed in Europe by encouraging more older people and women to stay in the workforce . Next week, the Commission will present revised proposals to meet the 2010 target to be quietly dropped by the end of the year . The report was commissioned by the European Commission to assess how far the EU has gone .
