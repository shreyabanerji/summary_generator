 Hyundai to build new India plant to meet growing demand for cars . South Korea's top car maker estimates that the Indian market will grow 15% this year, to 920,000 vehicles, reaching 1.6 million vehicles by 2010 . The new plant will be built close to the existing one in Chennai, in the southern province of Tamil Nadu . The company plans to use India as a base for exports to Europe, Latin America and the Middle East . It aims to become a global top five auto maker by 2010.
