 The two countries have signed a trade agreement with the U.S. It will allow Egyptian goods made in partnership with Israeli firms free access to American markets . The deal is a sign of a thaw in relations between the two countries . It will give a boost to Egyptian textile exports, which are about to suffer a drop after new regulations come into force in the US at the beginning of the year . Both countries exchanged prisoners earlier this month, with Egypt handing back an Israeli who has served eight years in prison after being convicted .
