 A barrel of light crude ended Monday down $1.33 to $42.12 a barrel in New York . However crude prices have rebounded in Asia, rising in February . In London, trading of Brent crude was suspended for a public holiday, but the price fell to $39.20 in the Far East . Milder temperatures expected to continue in the northern parts of the US over the next few days . Despite a fall of more than $12 from record highs reached in late October, the price of crude oil remains almost 30% higher than year-ago levels .
