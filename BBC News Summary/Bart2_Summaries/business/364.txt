 SSL International refused to comment on reports it may be subject to takeover . Financial Times report said GPW was starting due diligence work on SSL International . Shares in SSL up more than 6% or 16.75 pence to 293.5p . Analysts have seen SSL as a potential takeover target for years . Other potential suitors include Kimberly-Clark, Johnson & Johnson, and private equity investors . SSL was formed by a three-way merger between Seton Healthcare, Scholl and London International Group .
