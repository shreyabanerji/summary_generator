 The Bank of England has decided to keep interest rates at 4.75% . The decision was made by an eight-member committee of the Treasury . The rate is expected to be increased to 5% in the coming weeks . However, it is not likely that the rate will be increased until the end of the year, according to the bank's latest report . The bank is looking at the possibility of an increase in interest rates in the UK's highest rate of interest rates for the first time since 2008 .
