 Absa said it was close to striking a deal with Barclays in November . Barclays is still waiting for the approval of South Africa's banking and competition authorities to make a formal offer . If Barclays buys a stake in Absa it will be one of the largest foreign investments in South Africa in recent years . Absa also announced that it expects to see earnings grow by 20-25% in its current financial year . The South African group will release its results for the year to 31 March on 30 May .
