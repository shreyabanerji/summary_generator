 Police detain three top executives at Chinese milk firm Yili . Reports suggest they are being investigated for embezzlement . Yili spokesman says it may now move to oust chairman Zheng Junhuai . The official Xinhua News Agency said the arrest was linked to alleged embezlement . The company's shares were suspended on Tuesday, having fallen by 10% on Monday . The scandal is the latest to befall Chinese companies this year, with investors wondering if it is the tip of the iceberg .
