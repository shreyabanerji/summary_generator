 UK children use "pester power" to get their parents to stump up nearly a third of their annual income . The bulk goes on personal care, soft drinks and food, according to the report . British teenage girls are the most keen to use make-up products compared to their counterparts in seven European countries . The good times are likely to keep rolling for British children, as the report predicts that they will still be topping the earnings table in 2008, the report says . British children enjoy the highest average annual income in Europe .
