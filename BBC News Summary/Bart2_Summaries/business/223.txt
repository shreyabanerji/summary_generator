 Six UK greyhound tracks put up for sale by gaming group Wembley . US gaming consortium BLB will pay $339m (£182.5m) for the US unit . Analysts have valued the six tracks at between £40m-£50m . The sale of the US gaming unit will leave Wembley with its UK business . Wembley sold the English national football stadium in 1999 to concentrate on its gaming operations . Shares in Wembley were up 56 pence, or 7.6%, at 797p by mid-morning .
