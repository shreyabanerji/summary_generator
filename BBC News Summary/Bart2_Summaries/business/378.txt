 Supplies from Turkmenistan account for 45% of all natural gas imported by Ukraine . Turkmen President Saparmurat Niyazov said the Turkmen side agreed to lower the price demanded by $2 per 1,000 cubic metres, bringing it down to $58. But the new price is still $14 higher than the price fixed in the contract for 2004 . The head of the Ukrainian state-owned Naftohaz company said he was "fully happy" with the deal .
