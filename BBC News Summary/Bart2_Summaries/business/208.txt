 China's Shanda Interactive Entertainment buys stake in Sina, the country's biggest internet portal firm . Analyst says move may be a precursor to a full takeover . Rumours about a possible takeover boosted Sina's shares by more than 10% on Friday . A merger would create a firm that offers online role-playing games, news, entertainment and wireless messaging . Shanda said that the purchase of a stake by Shanda would have no impact on its business . However, analysts say a foreign company trying to take control of a Chinese internet portal may not be viewed very favourably by Beijing .
