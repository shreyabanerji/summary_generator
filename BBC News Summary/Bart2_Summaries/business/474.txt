 Virgin Blue shares plummet 20% after it warns of steep fall in full year profits . The airline, 25% owned by Richard Branson, has been struggling to fend off pressure from rival Jetstar . Virgin Blue was launched four years ago and now has roughly one third of Australia's domestic airline market . In November, first half profits were down due to slack demand and rising fuel costs . Shares in its major shareholder, Patrick Corporation, dropped 31% by the close of Wednesday's trading session . It cut its full year passenger number forecast by 2.5% .
