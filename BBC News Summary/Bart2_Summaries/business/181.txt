 India and Russia to work together in a series of energy deals . Gazprom and India's ONGC to lead oil and gas extraction deals . Indian firm keen on buying 15% stake in oil unit Yuganskneftegas . ONGC could invest in gas and oil fields in Sakhalin and may also take part in joint tender bids for projects in eastern Siberia and the Caspian Sea . India urgently searching for fresh energy supplies as domestic demand is growing at more than 5% a year . Agreement likely to see the two companies develop refining facilities in Russia, India and elsewhere and organise delivery of oil, gas and petrochemicals from Russia to India and other countries across Asia .
