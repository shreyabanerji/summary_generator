 Workers will be allowed to request to stay on beyond the age of 65 . The default age will not be a statutory, compulsory retirement age . The British Chambers of Commerce welcomed the move . Age Concern said imposing a retirement age was a 'complete u-turn' The decision will have no impact on the age at which workers can collect their state pension . It is now inevitable that older people will mount legal challenges to the decision, the group said . The move will not affect the state pension amount .
