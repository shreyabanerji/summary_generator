 Firms led by the Oil & Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC) will also assist in the development of Iranian oil fields . India imports about 70% of its total oil consumption . Iran is Opec's second-biggest oil producer and one of the world's top gas producers . It will supply India with 7.5 million tonnes of LPG annually over a 25 year period from 2009 . ONGC said it was in talks to buy former assets of troubled Russian oil firm Yukos .
