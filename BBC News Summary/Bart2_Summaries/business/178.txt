 Talks on Russia's proposed membership of the World Trade Organisation have been "making good progress" Russia has been waiting for a decade to join the WTO and hopes to become a member by early 2006 . Decision could be reached in December, when the WTO's 148 current members gather for a summit in Hong Kong . Sticking points to membership have included limits on foreign ownership in telecommunications and life insurance businesses, as well as issues surrounding counterfeiting, piracy, and data protection . President Bush said for Russia to make progress the government must "renew a commitment to democracy and the rule of law"
