 India's Air Deccan has signed a deal to acquire 36 planes from Avions de Transport Regional (ATR) The value of the deal has not been revealed, because of a confidentiality clause in the agreement . India's first low-cost airline recently ordered 30 Airbus A320 planes for $1.8bn . The potential of the Indian budget market has attracted attention from businesses such as Richard Branson and Vijay Mallya recently set up Kingfisher Airlines, while UK's Branson is keen to start a local operation .
