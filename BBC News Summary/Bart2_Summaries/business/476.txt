 Shares in Saudi Arabia's National Company for Cooperative Insurance (NCCI) soared 84% above the offer price on Monday . Demand for the insurer's debut shares was strong - 12 times what was on sale . The listing was part of the country's plans to open up its insurance market and boost demand in the sector . Deregulation is expected to boost demand for accident and damage cover . Saudi Arabia sold 7 million NCCI shares, or about 70% of the company's total capital last month .
