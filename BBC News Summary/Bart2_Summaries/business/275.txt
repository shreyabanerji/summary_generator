 Unemployment has not been this high in Germany since the 1930s . Bad winter weather also took its toll, as construction sector laid off workers . The opposition says there are a further 1.5 million-2 million people on subsidised employment schemes who are looking for work . The figures are embarrassing for the government, as they could put an end to the SPD's recent success in the polls, and with state elections due in Schleswig-Holstein and North Rhine-Westphalia .
