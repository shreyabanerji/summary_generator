House prices show slight increase

Prices of homes in the UK rose a seasonally adjusted 0.5% in February, says the Nationwide building society. Nationwide said that in January house prices went up by 0.4% on the month and by 12.6% on a year earlier. " We are not seeing the market collapsing in the way some had feared," said Nationwide economist Alex Bannister. Homeowners now expect house prices to rise by 1% over the next six months, Mr Bannister said. "I think the key is what the Bank expects to happen to the housing market.
