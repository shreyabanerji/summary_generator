EC calls truce in deficit battle

The European Commission (EC) has called a truce in its battle with France and Germany over breaching deficit limits. The move came after France and Germany vowed to run their budget deficits below the EU cap in 2005 - for the first time in four years. France and Germany will breach that this year. The forecasts are based on EC predictions of GDP growth of 1.5% in Germany next year and 2.2% in France.
