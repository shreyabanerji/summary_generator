Golden rule 'intact' says ex-aide

Chancellor Gordon Brown will meet his golden economic rule "with a margin to spare", according to his former chief economic adviser. Gordon Brown's decision to announce the date of the Budget while on a trip to China was a "sensible thing to do", since he was talking about skills and investment at the time, Mr Balls told the BBC. Expectations of a rate rise have gathered pace after figures showed that house prices are still rising.
