Green reports shun supply chain

Nearly 20% more UK top 250 firms produced non-financial reports on social and environment issues than last year. Blake Lee-Harwood, campaigns director at Greenpeace in the UK, said: " Less than a quarter of companies (24%) get their corporate social responsibility (CSR) reports independently verified to provide assurances they are accurate and complete, says the survey. The reports surveyed by Directions are published voluntarily.
