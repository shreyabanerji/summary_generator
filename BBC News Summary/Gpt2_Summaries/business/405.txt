House prices rebound says Halifax

UK house prices increased by 1.1% in December, the first monthly rise since September, the Halifax has said. Last week, the Nationwide said that house prices fell 0.2% in December, with annual inflation running at a three year low. The bank said that the recent pattern of house prices rising the fastest in the north of England continued in December. At the other send of the scale, prices in the South East and London fell by 1.6% and 0.5% respectively. This was the smallest quarterly rise since the second quarter of 2000, the bank said.
