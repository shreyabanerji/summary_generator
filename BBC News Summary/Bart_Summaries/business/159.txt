India to boost spending on primary schools and health in budget. Defence budget raised 7.8% to 830bn rupees ($19bn) Finance Minister Palaniappan Chidambaram's priority is to fight poverty. New law makes him cut the budget deficit to 4.5% of GDP in the year to March 2005. But heavy spending on poverty reduction means the 2005-6 target for the deficit will be 4.3%, he says. India's overall deficit is thought to be more than 10%, if the spending of India's 35 states is included.
