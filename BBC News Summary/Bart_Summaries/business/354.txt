Thailand's economy is now expected to grow by 5.7% in 2005, rather than 6% as forecast before tsunamis hit six tourist provinces. Indonesian, Indian and Hong Kong stock markets reached record highs on Wednesday. Investors seem to feel that some of the worst-affected areas were so under-developed that the tragedy would little impact on Asia's listed companies. However, many experts have pointed out that it is still difficult to assess the magnitude of the disaster and its likely economic impact.
