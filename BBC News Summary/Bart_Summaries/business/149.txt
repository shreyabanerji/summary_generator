Saab to build Cadillacs in Sweden as part of GM's efforts to appeal to European drivers. New car will be the first Cadillac with a diesel engine. Announcement should go some way to allay fears of the Saab factory's closure. Other luxury car makers, most notably the Toyota subsidiary Lexus, have enjoyed tremendous success in the US without managing to make significant inroads in Europe. Saab has consistently said it is competing head-on with BMW, Mercedes and Jaguar.
