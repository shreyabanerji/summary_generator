Dow Jones closes more than 1% higher at 10,137, while Nasdaq rises 0.9% to 2,004. Many investors believe that Mr Bush's policies are more business-friendly than those of his Democrat challenger, John Kerry. Higher share prices also reflect relief that a clear winner has emerged from what proved to be a tight poll. Some analysts predicted that the jump in share prices would be short-lived, saying investors would quickly focus again on the health of the US economy.
