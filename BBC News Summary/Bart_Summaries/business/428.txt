The Karachi Stock Exchange (KSE) recorded its largest single day gain, surging 3.5% to a new high. Energy and telecommunication stocks performed particularly well, recording an 8%-10% rise since Monday morning. The index rose 225.79 points in four hours of furious trading, with many investors optimistic that political stability could bring an economic boom. In 2002, the KSE was the world's best performing stock market, with the index rising 112%.
