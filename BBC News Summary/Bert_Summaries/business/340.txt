Venezuela and China sign oil deal

Venezuelan president Hugo Chavez has offered China wide-ranging access to the country's oil reserves. In 2004, according to forecasts from the Ministry of Commerce, China's oil imports will be 110m tons, up 21% on the previous year. Mr Chavez, who is visiting China, said his country would put its many of its oil facilities at the disposal of China. The two countries will also continue a joint venture agreement to produce stocks of the boiler fuel orimulsion.
