Bush to outline 'toughest' budget

President Bush is to send his toughest budget proposals to date to the US Congress, seeking large cuts in domestic spending to lower the deficit. Defence spending will rise, however, while the proposals exclude the cost of continuing military operations in Iraq. Also not featuring in the proposals is the cost of funding the administration's radical proposed overhaul of social security provision. However, Democratic Senator Kent Conrad said the proposals exposed the country to huge financial commitments beyond 2009. "
