US retail sales surge in December

US retail sales ended the year on a high note with solid gains in December, boosted by strong car sales. Sales climbed 8% for the year, the best performance since an 8.5% rise in 1999, the Commerce Department added. Sales for the year also broke through the $4 trillion mark for the first time - with annual sales coming in at $4.06 trillion However, if automotives are excluded from December's data, retail sales rose just 0.3% on the month.
