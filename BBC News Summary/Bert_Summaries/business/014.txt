Telegraph newspapers axe 90 jobs

The Daily and Sunday Telegraph newspapers are axing 90 journalist jobs - 17% of their editorial staff. The National Union of Journalists has called on the management to recall the notice of redundancy by midday on Monday or face a strike ballot. The brothers are currently mulling the sale of another of their businesses, retailer Littlewoods. However, action to improve our production capability and secure our titles against the competition is also vital." In recent months News Corp's News International unit, which publishes The Sun and the News of the World, the Guardian Media Group, Trinity Mirror and the Daily Mail & General Trust have all announced substantial investments in new printing plants.
