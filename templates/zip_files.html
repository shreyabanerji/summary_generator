Summary

A BJP MP from Himachal Pradesh, Ram Swaroop Sharma, was found dead at his residence in Delhi today, the police said, adding they are investigating whether it was a case of suicide. He was taken to a hospital where doctors pronounced him dead on arrival. Police officials said that Mr Sharma's personal assistant alerted the police control room around 7.45 am after the parliamentarian did not respond to knocks on his door. His body is being taken for postmortem," Mr Thakur was quoted as saying by news agency ANI.Mr Sharma was a two-time MP.He was elected to the Lok Sabha from the Mandi parliamentary constituency in 2014 and 2019.. The circumstances of the death are in the initial stages of investigation, police added."It looks like a case of suicide, but we are continuing to probe. A large quantity of medicines have been found from inside his room. Party officials and some people close to him have left for Delhi.
